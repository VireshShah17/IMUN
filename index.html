About Event

Embark on a Journey of Global Diplomacy: Discover SVVVIMUN Welcome to a realm where diplomacy knows no bounds, where young minds become the architects of change, and where the world converges in the spirit of collaboration and unity. Presenting the Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidhyalaya International Model United Nations (SVVVIMUN), a transformative 3-day odyssey of intellect, discourse, and leadership.

Immerse yourself in the heart of Indore, India, as SVVVIMUN unfurls its vibrant tapestry of international diplomacy. Set against the backdrop of the prestigious Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidhyalaya, this extraordinary event welcomes participants to step into the shoes of diplomats hailing from diverse corners of the globe.

Experience the world through the lens of diplomacy. Join us at the Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidhyalaya International Model United Nations and embark on a journey that will redefine your perspective, enhance your skills, and inspire you to become a catalyst for change on the global stage. The future of diplomacy starts here, with you, at SVVVIMUN.
