WHY SVVV INTERNATIONAL MUN?

Embark on a Journey of Global Diplomacy: Discover SVVVIMUN Welcome to a realm where diplomacy knows no bounds, where young minds become the architects of change, and where the world converges in the spirit of collaboration and unity. Presenting the Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidhyalaya International Model United Nations (SVVVIMUN), a transformative 3-day odyssey of intellect, discourse, and leadership.

SVVVIMUN is not just an event; it is a crucible for leadership development. Here, the art of public speaking is honed to perfection, transforming ordinary words into powerful instruments of change. Embark on a journey of meticulous research, where knowledge is your compass, guiding you through the intricate web of international relations. As you navigate through the twists and turns of diplomacy, you will emerge not only as a global citizen but as a poised and influential leader.

Experience the world through the lens of diplomacy. Join us at the Shri Vaishnav Vidhyapeeth Vishwavidhyalaya International Model United Nations and embark on a journey that will redefine your perspective, enhance your skills, and inspire you to become a catalyst for change on the global stage. The future of diplomacy starts here, with you, at SVVVIMUN.

ABOUT SVVV

Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya is a private university established under Madhya Pradesh Niji Vishwavidyalaya ( Sthapana Avam Sanchalan) Adhiniyam in 2015 at Indore (India). The University has been established with a vision to be leader in shaping better future for mankind through quality education, training and research. It shall pursue the mission to make a difference in sustaining the growth of global societies by developing socially responsible citizens. Value based education being at the helm, the University shall promote endurance, excellence, fairness, honesty and transparency as its core values.


Shri Vaishnav Vidyapeeth Vishwavidyalaya at Indore will be a multi-faculty University focusing on the needs of various segments of the society.
